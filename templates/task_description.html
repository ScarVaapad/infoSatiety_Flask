Task Description

In this study, you are asked to read 8 interactive scatterplots. The initial stage you see will be as followed:

You are expected to click on "Request more Data" button to acquire more data to reveal on the scatterplot.

Note: once you click to request more data, there is no going back.

When you feel the number of data points are good enough to predict a general trend (straight line) of the dataset, you can click on "Draw the Line" button to draw your estimation line(red).

Note: once you decide to draw (and click the button), you cannot request more data.

When you feel your line can well represent the underlying data set, you can click on "Submit" to submit your answer.

Note: once you click on "Submit" button, you can no longer draw for the case.

Your drawn line, then is compared against the true regression line of the underlying dataset. And you reward (apart from base payment) will be decide by:

  • How accurate your drawn line (red) aligns with the true regression line (blue)
  • How efficient you use data (meaning the less data used the higher the reward)
  • In tutorial mode, you will see a score (100 point-based) for your performance and a glimpse of the underlying dataset (true regression line), but you won't see the screen during your tasks.
